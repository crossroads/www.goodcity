Lead, Full Stack Programmer

You’ve got deep skills, years of experience and a genuine passion for technology. You’d love an opportunity to build something that will improve the lives of families in need and leave a lasting legacy.

Sound like you? We may have a job for you.

Details

Position Type: Full time

Remuneration: Competitive. Flexible based on experience

Location: On site in Hong Kong, Gold Coast area. Some remote work flexibility.

Attributes we're looking for:

  • At least eight years software engineering experience
  • Deep JavaScript experience and skills are essential
  • Demonstrable track record guiding architectural decisions at each layer of the stack
  • Ability to communicate well and lead a team (including offshore programmers)
  • Passion, positivity and empathy (building our team culture not taxing it)
  • Proficient in agile methods (test driven, iterative, SCRUM)
  • Experience with Ruby/Rails is advantageous
  • Experience with other elements in our stack is advantageous
  • Genuinely motivated by our vision and enthused by our values

Why you might want this job

  • Making a difference striving for a big impact not a big exit. If the work gets hard you’ll still be springing out of bed to get it done because extending a helping hand to thousands of people is motivating.
  • Getting to win this time : GoodCity is operated by the most established in-kind charity in Hong Kong and has already facilitated millions of dollars’ worth of goods donations. It has stellar partners, world-class leadership and three years of new development funding secured. GoodCity isn’t just a great idea hatched by well-intentioned people, it’s a growing success.
  • Working with great technology: See the diagram for a bird’s eye view of some tech and tools we use at the moment.
  • Having responsibility: You’ll work next to the project owners, decision makers and key users. You’ll lead a team of 2-3 other full-time developers and have a strong voice in shaping the technology you’re building.
  • Enjoying your job: Everyone's idea of their dream job differs but here are some things we think you'll like
    • People that you’ll enjoy working with (not ones that will wear you down)
    • Processes that are truly agile so you can actually reap the promised benefits
    • Pay that is competitive
